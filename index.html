Games for Change Team Project

Contamination

Don't let the pollutants take over the world!


Contamination is a game about saving the world from the pollution endangering our society. It's well known that pollution is a massive problem not only for our life and livelihoods, but for all living things around the world. Our game strives to be entertaining as well as to inform users of the pollution issue in our cities and communities.
